Overview

Purpose

This website is going to be web page for band. The purpose of the page is for people to be able to connect with the band by listening to their music as well as be able to show when their next shows are going to be.

Audience

The audience is going to be for people who like to listen to Indie music and are looking for local bands to go see. They will be local to the area so that they can come and watch the shows.

Dynamic elements

There will be a couple dynamic elements for the site. One dynamic element will be a Spotify player that is linked to the band's Spotify page. Another dynamic element will be an object array that will show when the band's upcoming shows are as well as the location of the shows.
